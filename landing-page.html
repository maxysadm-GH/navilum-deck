The Problem

53 million caregivers spend
8+ hours every week on healthcare admin

Navigating the cancer care system is a full-time job — on top of the job of actually caring for someone you love.

📞

Endless Phone Calls

Scheduling appointments, fighting insurance, tracking down lab results, chasing pharmacy refills — hours on hold every single week.

💊

Medication Chaos

Printed sheets, handwritten notes, conflicting schedules, missed doses. Managing a cancer patient's medication regimen is a dangerous puzzle.

👨‍👩‍👧

Family Coordination Burnout

Texting everyone, checking calendars, confirming rides, updating siblings — the emotional and logistical load is exhausting.

"During my wife's cancer treatment, I realized — if this is overwhelming for a technologist, how does everyone else survive?"
— Max, Founder & Caregiver
The Solution

One app that handles it all

Navilum is your AI health assistant — scanning documents, making calls, coordinating family, and saving money on medications.

📷
Scan

Your Meds, Organized Instantly

Photo your medication sheet. We create your entire schedule, set reminders, and flag interactions — automatically.

🤖
Call

AI Calls On Your Behalf

Our AI calls your doctor's office, insurance provider, and pharmacy — handling pre-authorizations, referrals, and refills while you rest.

🗓️
Coordinate

Family Stays in Sync

Everyone's availability, checked automatically. Rides, meals, and support scheduled without 40 text messages. One shared view for the whole family.

💰
Save

Find the Cheapest Meds

We compare every pharmacy and find manufacturer coupons automatically. 90% of users save more than their entire subscription cost — every month.

How It Works

Simple enough for the hardest days

Three steps. That's all it takes to offload weeks of healthcare admin.

📄
Step 1

Scan Your Documents

Photograph medication sheets, insurance cards, discharge papers, or lab results. Our AI reads and organizes everything instantly.

🎙️
Step 2

Tell Us What You Need

Speak or type naturally. "Schedule chemo and coordinate rides" or "Fight that insurance denial" — we understand context, not commands.

✅
Step 3

We Handle the Rest

AI makes the calls, tracks the medications, coordinates family schedules, and keeps you informed — without adding to your load.
