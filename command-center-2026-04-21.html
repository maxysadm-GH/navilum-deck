Anticipated questions — and our answers

"You're not HIPAA-compliant?" Correct, by design. We're a scheduler — users enter their own meds, we touch no PHI. v2 adds the provider dashboard with full HIPAA architecture. Investor-positive: ship now, not in 18 months. "Why won't Apple Reminders / MyTherapy / Medisafe just add escalating notifications?" Provisional patent on the escalation engine + chemo-cycle scheduling. Even without enforcement, it gates incumbent product roadmaps. And none of them have caregiver-swarm fan-out wired to a vault and a real schedule. "What's traction?" Internal alpha only. Brenda is the founder use case. We have not run external acquisition yet — that's a use-of-funds line. We are deliberately not quoting fabricated user counts. "Why a SAFE and not priced?" Per Ned (corporate counsel, 4/17): Chicago norms support $500K–$1M SAFE → $1.5–3M priced seed. Keeps cap table clean, defers valuation pressure until clinic pilot data. "What kills this?" A large EHR vendor adding caregiver alerts inside MyChart. Mitigation: the patent + the offline-first experience + the consumer brand. We're betting incumbents won't ship a delightful caregiver app.
