Legal / Immigration

Questions for Shelly Principe (E-2 Attorney)

Entity Structure

  • Can I form a health-tech subsidiary under MBACIO LLC without a new E-2 petition?
  • At what point do I need to file an amended I-129? Before first revenue? Before first employee?
  • Should we go Subsidiary (Path C) now and restructure to Holding Company (Path A) when VC investors need a clean cap table?
  • Does a DBA work for now, or does it need to be a separate LLC from day one?
  • What's the risk if the new venture becomes larger than MBACIO? Does USCIS care about relative size?

Fundraising & Investors

  • Can I accept investor money (SAFE notes) on the health-tech subsidiary while on E-2 through MBACIO?
  • Does taking outside investment dilute my ownership below E-2 requirements?
  • If investors require Delaware incorporation, how does that affect my Illinois-based E-2?
  • Can I be CEO/operator of the new venture and still meet "develop and direct" for MBACIO?
  • SBIR grants require 50%+ US citizen equity — does this conflict with E-2?

Timeline & Risk

  • When is my next E-2 renewal? How far in advance do we need to file amendments?
  • What documentation should I maintain to prove MBACIO remains "active and revenue-generating"?
  • If I hire US employees for the health venture, does that help or hurt the E-2 case?
  • What's the worst-case scenario if I proceed without filing amendments?

Patent & IP

  • Should the provisional patents be filed under MBACIO LLC, the new subsidiary, or my personal name?
  • If patents are filed under MBACIO, can they be assigned to a new entity later without issues?
  • Does patent ownership affect entity valuation for E-2 purposes?

FDA / Device Classification (for Patent Lawyer)

  • The app is an AI administrative assistant (scheduling, reminders, coordination, adherence logging) — confirm this is NOT an FDA-regulated medical device under Jan 2026 guidance.
  • The Gold tier adds a voice-controlled smart speaker for medication reminders and voice check-ins. It does NOT diagnose, treat, or independently monitor vitals. Is this consumer electronics (like Alexa) or does it risk FDA Class I/II classification?
  • If we later add optional RPM peripherals (blood pressure cuff, pulse ox) that pair with our speaker — does the speaker itself get reclassified, or only the peripherals carry the FDA burden?
  • For trademark filing: should we file the app in Class 9 (software) + Class 42 (SaaS), and the speaker in Class 9 (electronics)? Or do we need Class 10 (medical) for any of it?
  • How should we word patent claims to protect the hardware tier without triggering medical device language? (e.g., "wellness reminder device" vs "health monitoring device")
