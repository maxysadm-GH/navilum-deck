What we will NOT do in 6 months

  • 📵 No paid acquisition before pilot data — burn discipline matters
  • 🚫 No HIPAA-required v1 features in consumer app — keep MVP outside HIPAA scope; build it in v2 for the provider surface only
  • 📉 No premium price experiments before 1K paid users — preserve signal
  • 🏥 No more than 2 clinic pilots before product is proven — concentrated learning over distributed noise
  • 👥 No FTE hires beyond engineer #1–2 before SAFE closes
  • 📰 No press push until App Store reviews + first pilot retention data
