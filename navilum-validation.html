💡 Brand Status: Domains Secured, Clearance Pending

⚠ Key Positioning: Administrative Tool, NOT Medical Device
Must be positioned as an AI administrative assistant for healthcare, NOT a medical device. This avoids FDA 510(k) jurisdiction entirely per January 2026 guidance. Frame all features around scheduling, communication, and document management — never diagnosis or treatment recommendations. The Gold tier smart speaker is consumer electronics (like Alexa), not a medical device.
