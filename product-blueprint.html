Section 01

Platform Overview

Who we are, who we serve, and why patient-owned is the only model that scales.

This platform is an AI-powered health command center that lives on the patient's side — not the provider's. It reads medication sheets with OCR, schedules appointments by checking the whole family's calendar, makes phone calls to insurers on the patient's behalf, and turns a mountain of fragmented healthcare paperwork into a single, calm, voice-accessible dashboard. Whether a patient is navigating a new cancer diagnosis, managing five daily medications for a chronic condition, or simply an elderly parent who keeps missing refills, this platform becomes their trusted health co-pilot.

Unlike provider-facing tools (Epic, Athena, Veradigm), we own the patient relationship 24/7. Providers see a patient for 18 minutes a year. We are with that patient every day.

🎯 Key Differentiator: Patient-side, not provider-side — we own the daily relationship

Technical Architecture

Azure-native, HIPAA-ready from day one. Every vendor has a BAA. Every data store is encrypted.

⚛️
Frontend
PWA — React / Next.js
Deployed on Azure App Service. Progressive web app for iOS + Android without app store friction. Offline-capable for reminders.
🎙️
Voice AI
Retell AI
HIPAA BAA included. Outbound + inbound call handling, natural language understanding, real-time transcription.
🔍
OCR & Document AI
Azure AI Document Intelligence + Mindee
Pre-built models for insurance cards, lab reports, and Rx sheets. Mindee as fallback for non-standard formats.
📱
SMS / Messaging
Twilio (HIPAA Security Edition)
BAA signed. Used for family SMS coordination, caregiver alerts, and two-factor authentication flows.
📅
Calendar Integration
Google Calendar API + Microsoft Graph
Bidirectional sync for appointment creation and conflict detection across patient and family accounts.
💾
Storage & Database
Azure Blob (Encrypted) + Supabase HIPAA
Documents in Azure Blob with customer-managed keys. Structured data in Supabase with HIPAA add-on and row-level security.
⚙️
Orchestration / Automation
Self-hosted n8n on Azure VM
All workflow automation — reminder triggers, call scheduling, escalation chains. Fully auditable, no vendor lock-in.
🛡️
Auth & Identity
Supabase Auth + Azure AD B2C
Row-level security enforced at the database layer. Azure AD B2C for enterprise SSO integrations.

Section 05

HIPAA Compliance Architecture

Compliance is not an afterthought. Every architectural decision was made with HIPAA first.

🔐

End-to-End Encryption

TLS 1.3 for all data in transit. AES-256 for all data at rest. Customer-managed keys for document vault.

📑

Business Associate Agreements

BAA executed with every vendor that touches PHI: Retell AI, Twilio, Supabase, Azure, and all third-party integrations.

🚫

Zero PHI Retention

Transcriptions processed and encrypted within seconds. No PHI retained in AI model training pipelines or logs.

📋

Comprehensive Audit Logging

Every data access, modification, and export logged immutably. Azure Monitor + Supabase audit trails retained 7 years.

🎭

Role-Based Access Control

Database-layer row-level security. Patient, family-viewer, family-helper, caregiver, and provider roles with distinct permission scopes.

🏅

HITRUST e1 Certification Path

Gap assessment planned Month 6. HITRUST e1 certification targeted by Month 18. Unlocks hospital and payer enterprise contracts.

Section 06

Competitive Moat Timeline

The moat is not a feature. It is the compounding of data, relationships, and switching costs over time.

Month 0 – 6
First-Mover Advantage
No direct competitor combines OCR-parsed medication sheets, voice-first UX, and AI outbound calling in a single patient-owned app. Early adopters — particularly cancer patients navigating diagnosis — become highly loyal. Caregiver word-of-mouth is the primary acquisition channel.
App + OCR Voice UX AI Call Agent Free Tier Habit BELC Foundation
Moat Strength
Low
Month 6 – 12
Network Effects Kick In
Families invite families. When a patient invites three family members who each manage a parent of their own, the user base compounds organically. Family accounts create shared dependency — leaving means leaving everyone. Referral-driven CAC drops below $15 per acquired user, making paid acquisition economics exceptional.
Family Invite Loop Multi-Patient Mgmt CAC < $15 Caregiver Word-of-Mouth
Moat Strength
Med
Month 12 – 18
The Revenue Flywheel
Scale in users unlocks better pharmacy partnership rates, higher pharma CPMs, and preferred clinical trial referral agreements. Better economics allow more feature investment, driving retention, driving more users. RPM enrollment with clinical partners begins — unlocking the $140–$210/month reimbursement revenue stream. This is the inflection point.
RPM Revenue Unlock Pharma Partnerships Pharmacy Rates Clinical Trial Fees
Moat Strength
High
Month 18 – 24
Deep Integration Moat
Hardware lock-in (smart speakers in homes), 18+ months of medication history, established family coordination workflows, and HITRUST certification enabling hospital contracts. Switching costs become prohibitive — all health history, family connections, pharmacy integrations, and 2 years of adherence data would be lost. Churn drops below 2% monthly.
