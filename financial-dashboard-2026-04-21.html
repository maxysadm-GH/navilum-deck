Premium unlocks: caregiver network, escalation rules, voice-clone alarm, vault, side-effect correlation panel.

Rule: Reminders are never paywalled. Free tier saves lives. Paid tier saves families.

What we will NOT do

No ad-supported tier (caregivers + patients are not an ad surface). No data sale without explicit per-record consent. No paywall on the reminder itself.
